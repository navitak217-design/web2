Article (Definition)

An article is a word used before a noun to show whether the noun is specific or general.
Examples: a, an, the

Types of Articles

There are two types of articles:

Indefinite Articles (A, An)

Used when we talk about something general or for the first time.

Use of “A”

Before words that start with a consonant sound
Examples: a book, a pen a university

Use of “An”

Before words that start with a vowel sound (a, e, i, o, u)
Examples: an apple, an hour, an umbrella
Note: Sound matters, not spelling.

Definite Article (The)

Used when we talk about something specific or already known.
Examples: the sun, the teacher, the book on the table

Rules of Articles

Use a/an with singular countable nouns
:-Use the when the noun is specific
:-Do not use articles before:

Examples Sentences

I have a pen.
She ate an apple.
The teacher is in the class.
