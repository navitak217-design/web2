PART OF SPEECH

On your grammar website, you can add the following Parts of Speech with short definitions and simple examples:

1. Noun

A noun is the name of a person, place, thing, or idea. Examples: boy, city, book, honesty

2. Pronoun

A pronoun is used in place of a noun to avoid repetition. Examples: he, she, it, they, we

3. Verb

A verb shows an action or a state of being. Examples: run, eat, write, is, are

4. Adjective

An adjective describes a noun or pronoun. Examples: big, beautiful, smart, red

5.Adverb

An adverb describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb. It often tells how, when, where, or how much. Examples: quickly, very, yesterday, here

6. Preposition

A preposition shows the relationship between a noun/pronoun and another word. Examples: in, on, under, between, behind

7. Conjunction

A conjunction joins words, phrases, or sentences. Examples: and, but, because, or, although

8. Interjection

An interjection expresses sudden feelings or emotions. Examples: wow, oh, hey, alas
